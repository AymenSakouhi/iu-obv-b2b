What documents are needed?

Upload the documents as listed below using the section above or send them to partners@iu.org:

  • Curriculum vitae: your up to date CV

  • Transcript of record

    A certified copy of your high school transcripts (if you are applying for a Bachelor’s degree) or your Bachelor transcripts (if you are applying for a Master’s degree) in the original language along with their official translation into German or English

  • Highest academic certificate

    Clear scan of your graduation certificate from high school (if you are applying for a Bachelor’s degree) or Bachelor degree certificate (if you are applying for a Master’s degree) in the original language along with their official translation into German or English

  • ID/Passport

  • Proof of English proficiency

    TOEFL, IELTS or Duolingo or take our new Speexx programme for free to reach the required English level

  • Proof of full-time professional work experience (for Master’s applicants only)

    A job reference letter or employer’s certificate with your start and end dates of employment. Note: internship certificates are not accepted. Only work experience after your Bachelor’s degree is counted.
